Peer Mentor Program

Our college peer mentoring idea envisions the development of a comprehensive peer mentoring program tailored to the particular needs
and challenges encountered by college students. This program aims to provide students with essential support, guidance, and a feeling
of community in order to help them achieve academically and personally during their college experience. The primary purpose
of this proposal is to establish a structured mentoring system that encourages peer-to-peer help and enhances the
entire college experience by combining experienced upperclassmen (mentors) with new or less-experienced students (mentees).





Peer Mentors can assist with:

  • Connecting & networking with students and working professionals
  • Resume Help
  • Career Advice
  • Tutoring
  • Finding resources
